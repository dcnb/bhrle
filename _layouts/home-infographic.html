Black History @ the University of Idaho is an ongoing collaboration between the Black History Research Lab and the University of Idaho Library that examines the history and contribution of Black people at the U of I.

Directed by Dr. Sydney Freeman Jr., this digital exhibit space and the regular physical exhibits that feature similar explorations seeks to uncover untold histories and highlight the accomplishments of Black faculty, staff, and students.
